01 — THE CURRENT

What is Pravah?

Pravah — meaning "flow" — is SKIT's annual techno-cultural festival, where three days of energy converge into one unstoppable current. Students, faculty and staff step off the syllabus and into hackathons, stage battles, robotics arenas and open mics, all racing toward the same prize pool, the same stage, the same night.

Whether you write code, dance under lights, or just want to watch a treasure hunt spiral out of control across campus — Pravah has a lane for you. No spectators required. Everyone who registers becomes part of the current.
